Mary

Mary, the mother of Jesus Christ, is another key figure in Christianity known for her faith and humility. A young Jewish woman from Nazareth, she was chosen by God to conceive Jesus through the Holy Spirit, fulfilling prophecies of the Messiah’s birth. Mary remained a faithful presence throughout Jesus’ life, from His birth in Bethlehem to His crucifixion. She is honored in Christian tradition as a model of obedience and trust in God.
